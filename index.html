Common Questions

Answers to what most people ask before reaching out

How long does a typical project take?
Most MVPs and internal tools take 2–4 weeks depending on scope. A landing page + lead system can be done in under 2 weeks. We scope precisely before starting so you always know what to expect.
What's your pricing model?
We offer fixed-scope pricing for well-defined projects and retainer-based pricing for ongoing work. After your discovery call we provide a clear written quote — no hidden costs, no surprise invoices.
Do you work with early-stage startups or solo founders?
Yes — that's our focus. Early-stage teams and solo founders are exactly who we built Onixera for. We understand lean budgets and fast timelines, and we help you prioritize what actually needs to ship first.
Can I see examples of your previous work?
Absolutely. Book a discovery call and we'll walk you through relevant case snapshots, including the internal dashboard package and lead funnel system we've delivered for clients so far.
What if I only have a rough idea and no technical spec?
Perfect — that's where we start. Our discovery process is specifically designed to take a rough idea and produce a clear, prioritized scope. You don't need a spec sheet to get started with us.
Do you offer support after the project is delivered?
Yes. Every delivery includes documentation and a handover session. We also offer optional retainer plans for ongoing maintenance, feature additions, and support — so your product keeps improving.
